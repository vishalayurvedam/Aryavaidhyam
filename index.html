About

I am Dr. Vishal Maghrola, an Ayurvedic physician based in Dhandhuka, Gujarat, dedicated to helping people restore balance and vitality through the timeless science of Ayurveda. With over 3+ years of clinical experience, I combine classical Ayurvedic principles with a compassionate, personalised approach to healing.

My practice is rooted in the belief that true health goes beyond the absence of disease — it is a harmonious state of body, mind, and spirit. With hands-on experience in both OPD and IPD care, and having guided 1000+ patients, I bring clinical depth and genuine compassion to every consultation.
